07/23/2021: Sam Grendel

Sam Grendel is a saxophonist who takes a more experimental approch to jazz as his music often uses a varitey of different synths and drum machines to complement his Sax skills. 'Music for Saxofone and bass Guitar' is a 2018 album where he tries some interesting things with his sax.
